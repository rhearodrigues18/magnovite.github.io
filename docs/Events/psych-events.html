Psychology Events

  • SCHOOL OF THOUGHTS

    The event consists of two rounds - Quiz and Debate - based on the schools of psychology.

    Description and Rules

    We know there exists a lot of schools of psychology, each school has its strengths as well as weaknesses. So, to bring together all the schools of psychology together, an event where each school gets a chance to speak for its own foundation and strengths can be organized. A debate sort of environment where you can cross question any school, try to use your original thoughts and logic to provide a backup to any argument, ensuring that the argument in itself is formed on the facts. Multiple schools of psychology like behaviourism, psychoanalysis, structuralism etc. can be taken in, and further groups can be formed accordingly. The teams can form their own names, something incongruence with that particular school.

  • SCRIBBLED POSTS

    Participants will have to come up with one or two lines containing a psychology-related pun in the form of an Instagram Post.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1.Event form- Submission based
    2.Participant format - 2 per group
    3.Objectives- Open Topic. A paragraph of minimum 25 words and maximum of 50 words must be created with at least 2 psychology related puns. Humor is appreciated. It is to be sent in pdf format.
    4.Points to remember- No derogatory, brash, targeted submissions will be tolerated. Avoid triggering statements that may invoke negative emotions.

  • BLACK-OUT POETRY

    A black out poetry is when the author scratches out and redacts words from an already established text to come up with poetry. John Caroll accidently invented this form of expression of emotions.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    Based on the number of the participants, breakout rooms will be formed with the volunteers present in each room.
    Volunteers will also provide any assistance required to the participants and resolve any technical issues required.
    Participants must keep their videos on and mute themselves. They can ask their queries in the chat box
    Participants must submit their work in the link posted in the chat box, once they are completed with it. The link will be provided in the chat box during the event.
    Participants need to mention their names at the right end of the page they work on.
    Duration of the event is 45 mins where participants will get 30 mins to perform the blackout poetry and submit within the given time. In the next 15 minutes, all the art pages will be presented.
